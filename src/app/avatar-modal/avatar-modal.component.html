Avatar Portal (Under Construction. There is no way back yet.)

Stepping through the avatar portal allows you to experience a new kind of rebirth, taking on a the role of a mortal with specific limitations.
This will strip you of all your possessions, your home, your wealth, most of your attributes and aptitudes, all pill effects, and nearly all of the capabilities you have acquired through your many lives.
You will retain your divine attributes, your achievements, your automatic behaviors, your Tao contemplation, and your understanding of the workings of the universe (game panels).
Each avatar challenge has a special goal that you will need to reach before you can resume your existence as a god.
There is no escape and no shortcut for returning to godhood without reaching this goal, so do not undertake these challenges lightly!
Avatar challenges persist across reincarnations, allowing you to attempt to reach your goal for as long as it takes until you reach it.
Entering an avatar challenge will also reset your relationships with all foreign gods, allowing you to defeat them again at their original strength.
Enemy strength will not reset on reincarnation during avatar challenges.
You should export your save file before proceeding!
{{BLOODTHIRSTY_BRAWLER}} Avatar
Take on the role of a mortal specially gifted in unarmed combat who loves nothing more than to fight. You will never take up arms during this avatar challenge, instead relying solely on your fists and feet. Enemy strength will not reset on reincarnation.
Goal: Defeat 1,000,000 Enemies
Reward: Lifesteal Technique Refinement

{{TREE_LOVER}} Avatar
Take on the role of a mortal who loves trees so much that you will never cut one down. During this avatar challenge, you can never chop wood or do anything else that might harm a tree, and neither will your followers. Enemy strength will not reset on reincarnation.
Goal: Create a wooden weapon with 8,000,000 Attack power
Reward: New kinds of wood and increased power for wooden weapons

Reward: New kinds of metal and increased power for metal weapons
